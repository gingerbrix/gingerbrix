The Store

👋 About GingerBrix

I'm a Lego hobbyist who builds custom MOCs and designs instructions for other fans to enjoy. GingerBrix is where I share my designs, 3D printed parts, and custom minifigures.

📐

MOC Instructions

Original Lego-compatible models with detailed PDF instructions. Every set is tested before release and delivered instantly by email.

🖨️

3D Printed Parts

Custom accessories designed to fit standard Lego minifigures. Helmets, weapons, and more — compatible and durable.

🧱

Minifigures

Custom-designed Lego-compatible minifigures with unique torso prints, accessories, and hair. Small runs, made with care.

📦

Wave Shipping

Physical orders ship in scheduled waves so every order gets packed carefully. Digital PDFs always arrive instantly.
